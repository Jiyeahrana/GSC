Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Delay Prediction Engine work? expand_more
Our engine ingests live AIS vessel data, historical transit times for specific carriers, real-time weather alerts, and port performance KPIs. Using a custom Transformer-based model, it calculates the ETA probability for every terminal on a shipment's route.
Can POMS integrate with our existing TOS? expand_more
Yes. POMS is built with a "Connect-First" philosophy. We offer robust API endpoints and webhooks that integrate seamlessly with major Terminal Operating Systems like Navis, Cosmos, and Tideworks.
What is the onboarding timeline for new ports? expand_more
A typical deployment takes 4–6 weeks, covering data mapping, API integration, and staff training. Our cloud-native architecture allows for rapid scaling without on-site hardware installation.
